Free Domains for Your XNo Website

One of the most exciting features of XNo is the ability to get a free domain for your self-hosted site within the XNo ecosystem. This empowers creators to own their unique web presence without the usual hassle or cost.

Instead of complex DNS setups or paying for expensive domain names, XNo allows you to have a custom folder domain on the main repository, resulting in URLs like:

  • yourxnosite.xno
  • awesomeproject.xno
  • coolblog.xno

These domains are simple, memorable, and fully integrated with the XNo hosting system.

How to Get Your Free Domain

There are two main ways to claim your free XNo domain:

  • Fork the main repository on GitHub and customize your own site — your domain will be based on your GitHub username and repo.
  • Submit a pull request to add a folder with your site under the main repository, which grants you a simple and branded domain like yourxnosite.xno.

The pull request approach lets you skip the forking step and still be part of the XNo family, making it easier than ever to publish your site.
